# Shrink auth showcase for 100% zoom

**Date:** 2026-07-21

## What changed

- Reduced showcase max width (`max-w-sm` / `xl:max-w-md`).
- Smaller headline, body, channel pills, inbox rows, and stats.
- Tighter padding and spacing throughout the right panel.
- Dropped one mock conversation row (3 instead of 4) to reduce vertical bulk.

## Why

At 100% zoom the live preview panel felt larger than the form column and unbalanced the split layout.
