# Refined professional auth polish

**Date:** 2026-07-21

## What changed

- Removed icon/text marquee backgrounds (felt messy and childish).
- Switched to white + slate-50 split panel with subtle dot grid and inbox lane lines.
- Unified channel pills and stats to neutral slate with single blue accent.
- Simplified brand typography (solid title + blue dot, no rainbow gradient).

## Why

User feedback: floating icons looked random; design felt childish, not clean B2B.
